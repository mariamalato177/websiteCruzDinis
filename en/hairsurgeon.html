Dr. Pedro Cruz Dinis

Meet Dr. Cruz Dinis, a doctor specialized in Hair Restoration.

Dr. Pedro Cruz Dinis



He began his career in Emergency Medicine at INEM. He transitioned to Aesthetic Medicine, but his passion for Hair Restoration was greater.

Born in Angola, he has lived on 4 continents, never stopping his travels in search of expanding and sharing his knowledge in the field of trichology and hair restoration surgery. He is also a member of various associations worldwide.

Out of his 30 years of experience as a doctor, 15 years have been dedicated to changing lives and boosting self-esteem because that is how he feels fulfilled!

Career Path

  • Started practicing hair transplants in 2007.
  • First Hair Transplant Course using the FUT technique in 2007 under the guidance of plastic surgeon Dr. Zoran Lekic.
  • Specialized in Hair Restoration using FUT, FUE, and BHT techniques in Bangkok, Thailand (2011), under the guidance of Dr. Viroj Vong, executive member of ISHRS (International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery).
  • Has performed hundreds of Hair Restorations using FUE, FUT, and BHT techniques.
  • Graduated in Medicine from the Faculty of Medicine of Lisbon in 1994.
  • Specialist in Occupational Medicine certified by the Medical Association.
  • Courses in the field of Medicine and Aesthetics.
  • Multiple courses in the field of Emergency Medicine.
  • Post-graduate degree as a Senior Technician in Occupational Health and Safety.
